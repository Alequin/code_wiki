
The site is a collection of knowledge which I personally add to. It acts as a
way to continue my learning in multiple areas and keep a record of that learning
for the future.

# Requirements
  - Have individual pages with titles, summaries and content
  - Be able to add pages with custom content
  - Be able to edit pages
  - Pages should record their creation date and last edited date
  - Pages should be able to hold multiple forms of content
    (text, image, code block, ect)
  - Pages should be able to have multiple searchable categories to make finding
    them easy.
  - Pages should be searchable
